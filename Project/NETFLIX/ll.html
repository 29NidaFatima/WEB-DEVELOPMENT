The Witcher: Season 2 (Teaser)

The Witcher is a fantasy drama series created by Lauren Schmidt Hissrich, based on the book series of the same name by Andrzej Sapkowski. The show is set in a fictional world known as the Continent and follows the story of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher. The teaser for Season 2 promises more action, adventure, and magic as Geralt embarks on new quests and encounters dangerous creatures.
